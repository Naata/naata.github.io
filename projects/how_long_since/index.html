How Long Since?

Developed as Dart language/Flutter framework excercise.
Had som much fun writing it and I’m actively using it on daily basis to keep track of these pesky tasks that pop up every now and then :)

From Google Play description:

Have you ever asked yourself a question - “How long since I did …?"
Like going to hairdresser - sure, you want to pay them a visit every two months, but one or two weeks later won’t change much :)
“How Long Since?” app helps you to track such tasks and mark them as “overdue” if needed. but without bothering you with notifications or aggressive reminders.
